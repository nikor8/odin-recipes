Instant Pot Mac n Cheese

An insanely easy meal to make for all those Instant Pot enthusiasts out there

Probably the easiest meal that we have presented! The Instant Pot does all of the work for you. All you have to do is dump the ingredients in, program the IP, and wait a short amount of time. How amazing is that?!
